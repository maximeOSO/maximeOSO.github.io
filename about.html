SG054

About

SG054 is a superconducting gravimeter hosted at the Onsala Space Observatory.

SG054 processed data (tide, air pressure, polar motion, drift) in microGal:

second sampling: sg_for_NKG_ICAG_2022_SECONDS.csv

minute sampling: sg_for_NKG_ICAG_2022_MINUTES.csv

minute sampling, with detail of each effect: aux_and_sg_for_NKG_ICAG_2022_MINUTES.csv (GRAV1 is the original gravity data, the last column is the processed data, same as in sg_for_NKG_ICAG_2022_MINUTES.csv above

Processed following Wziontek et al. (2021) with the local tide model given in the protocole (pdf).

Polar motions won't be correct before August 12 2022 (till then, approximated to cope with month-lag IERS).

Station info: Latitude: 57.39640 N, Longitude: 11.92590 E, Altitude: 7.50 m

The GIA effect is taken away with the drift estimation. It is about -0.30 +/- 0.07 microgal/year at Onsala (Olsson et al., 2019).
